About PRUFF

How it works?

AI based app that registers through IMEI number generating a Unique Identity Number, Permanently attached in the system storage, it allows to access location online and offline.

Using the GPS of device with an ampere of current, phone is accessible within 10-15 seconds of the device being turned on. PRUFF locates the device 6 times a minute i.e. every second.

All our subscribers need to do – type in the query on PRUFF portal and location will be mailed to registered email address for the span of 2 days.
